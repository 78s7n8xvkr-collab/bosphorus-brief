    Morning Çay

    —

    Morning Çay is the Brief's daily briefing-within-the-briefing. Once a day, on İstanbul time, an AI editor reads the entire feed — every outlet, every lens — and brews it down to a few hundred words on what actually matters for life here: money, paperwork, safety, and the day's big picture. When state-aligned, opposition, and international outlets tell the same story differently, it says so. It's a starting point, not a verdict — verify anything critical with the original reporting.
